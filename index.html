About us

We are a product development agency based in Kerala, operating remotely from Bangalore, specializing in both design and development services. From crafting user-centric product designs to building functional and scalable solutions, we offer end-to-end services including product design and development, web design and development, and more. Our fully remote approach allows us to collaborate seamlessly and deliver innovative solutions tailored to meet your unique needs.

Project Timeline

Define & plan

Our team prepares and presents comprehensive creative briefs, which include detailed product briefs, timelines, and deliverables, to ensure alignment and clarity with all stakeholders.

Design & Ideation

Our team designs the assets and final screens, which are then presented to the client.

Revision

The team will revise the designs based on client feedback and present the updated versions for further input.

Develop & Test

Once the main designs are finalised, our development team will begin development and testing. The completed solution will be presented to the client for review before deployment.

Payment

Before final delivery, the client will complete the final payment to us. In some cases, project payments will be structured in milestones, depending on the project.
